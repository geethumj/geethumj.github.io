Welcome to my personal webpage

I am a PhD scholar in Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India. I work in the area of Video Analytics, under the guidance of Prof. Sukhendu Das. My main interests are in the field of machine learning, optimization methods, computer vision and statistical learning theory. The specific problems that I work on in my thesis are Video Stabilization, Moving Object Segmentation and Panorama Creation on Unconstrained and Shaky Videos. I did my Masters in Cochin University of Science and Technology, Kochi, Kerala, India and Bachelors in TKM College of Engineering, Kollam, Kerala.

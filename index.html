Hi! I am a fifth year PhD scholar, at CSE department in Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India. I work with Prof. Sukhendu Das in the field of Video Analytics. My main interests are in the field of machine learning, optimization methods, computer vision, statistical learning theory and computational geometry. The specific problems that I work on in my thesis are Video Stabilization, Moving Object Segmentation and Panorama Creation on Unconstrained and Shaky Videos. I have been awarded TCS scholarship during the tenure of my thesis and have 6 publications associated with my thesis, including one journal.
